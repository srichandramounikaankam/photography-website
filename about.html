Dream Photography

A dream photographer is someone who captures the essence of dreams and the subconscious in their work. They might use surreal compositions, unconventional angles, and creative techniques to evoke the feeling of being in a dreamlike state.The goal is often to translate the abstract, often illogical nature of dreams into visual art. This could involve manipulating light, shadow, and color, or incorporating elements that defy the laws of reality.
